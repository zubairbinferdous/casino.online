If it is deposit-free, or the website does not have wagering requirements, consider it as an offer worth taking up as wagering-free spins are hard to come by.
  2. Value of the Free Spins
    Some online casino sites give the value of the free spins upfront. If they only gave the number of spins without the average cost per spin, do a manual calculation.     Most free spins are tied down to specific games. In such situations, the software developer uses free spins to lure new players to the game. However, other free spins are open, with basic conditions such as a minimum deposit required and bonus wagering requirement.

    It is also a way of introducing and increasing traffic on a payment method. Choose a free spins offer that uses your preferred payment method on the first deposit.
  5. Deposit or no deposit
    A free spins no deposit bonus is always worth taking a look at as it involves players picking up free spins without having to use their own funds.

Why do online casinos offer free spins?

Yes. However, bonus winnings from the free spins must undergo wagering requirements before they can be turned into real money. What’s the difference between free spins given from a bonus and free spins given in-game?

Free spins given from a bonus apply after an action or decision is made - reward for an activity. In this case, you have to do a task like make a deposit or free bets. It’s also worth noting that free spins offered by the casino will always come with their own wagering requirements, no matter what.
